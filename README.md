PC2Cloud revolutionizes personal data storage by turning any old computer—and all its connected drives—into an expandable private cloud. As users add more hard drives or devices to their system, their storage capacity grows seamlessly, creating a vast personal storage network that’s both free and entirely under their control. Unlike subscription cloud services with fixed limits, PC2Cloud automatically recognizes and incorporates new storage devices, giving users an affordable way to build massive storage pools from hardware they already own. The system requires zero technical setup—just plug in additional drives, and they instantly become part of your secure, accessible-from-anywhere cloud. This approach not only saves money but also reduces e-waste by breathing new life into old computers and unused hard drives. Perfect for media collectors, creative professionals, or anyone needing scalable storage without monthly fees, PC2Cloud delivers true ownership in an era of cloud monopolies.
